# Trusted Stranger Site Requirements

These are standing requirements for every future website update.

## Brand header

- Keep the Gide logo and `by Trusted Stranger` branding in the header of every page.
- The logo must link back to `https://trustedstranger.app/`.
- Do not replace the branded header with a plain text `Gide home` link.
- Preserve enough clear space around the logo for visibility and brand recognition.

## Footer

- Add the standard Trusted Stranger footer to every public HTML page.
- The footer should include the Gide brand, product links, books/resources, company links, support email, legal links, and copyright notice.
- Keep footer navigation and product availability accurate across every page.

## App destinations

Use these as the canonical public destinations for the Gide apps:

- Gide Guard: `https://guard.trustedstranger.app/`
- Gide Calm: `https://calm.trustedstranger.app/`
- Gide Faith: `https://faith.trustedstranger.app/`
- Gide Stride: `https://stride.trustedstranger.app/`
- Gide Legacy: `https://legacy.trustedstranger.app/`

Do not switch live links to a subdomain until its DNS and hosting are configured and the page has been verified. Until then, the corresponding `/apps/` page may remain as the working fallback.
