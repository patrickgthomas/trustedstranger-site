About Gide

Practical guidance for everyday life.

Gide is a growing family of apps, books, and guides created to help people make better decisions, stay safer, build better habits, and use AI with more confidence.

What Gide Is

Gide was created by Trusted Stranger as a simple idea: build useful tools that help people move through life with more guidance, clarity, and confidence.

The Gide family includes mobile apps for safety, calm, faith, and progress. It also includes plain-English AI books and resources for people who want to understand AI without feeling overwhelmed.

Everything is designed to be practical, easy to understand, and useful in real life. Gide is not about hype. It is about helping people take action.

Our Approach

Gide is built around a few simple values: privacy, clarity, usefulness, and trust. The goal is to create tools that are easy to use and easy to understand.

Many Gide tools are designed to work without requiring users to give up more information than necessary. The focus is on practical support, not collecting personal data.

Whether someone is reading a guide, opening an app, or looking for a quick answer, Gide aims to provide help that feels clear, calm, and useful.

Looking Ahead

Gide is still growing. New apps, books, tools, and resources may be added over time as the Gide family expands.

The long-term goal is to build a trusted collection of simple digital tools that help people live wiser, work smarter, stay safer, and move forward with confidence.
