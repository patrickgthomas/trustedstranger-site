About Gide

Practical guidance for everyday life.

Gide is a growing family of apps, books, and guides created to help people make better decisions, stay safer, build better habits, and use AI with more confidence.

What Gide Is

One brand built around guidance, support, and protection.

Gide was created by Trusted Stranger to provide practical tools for real life. The goal is simple: help people live wiser, work smarter, stay safer, and move forward with confidence.

The Gide family includes mobile apps for safety, calm, faith, and progress. It also includes plain-English AI books and resources for people who want to understand and use AI without feeling overwhelmed.

Gide is not about hype. It is about practical help, clear guidance, and useful tools people can use right away.

Our Approach

Clear, practical, and built for real people.

Gide is built around privacy, clarity, usefulness, and trust. The goal is to create tools that are easy to use and easy to understand.

Many Gide tools are designed to work without requiring users to give up more information than necessary. The focus is on practical support, not collecting personal data.

Whether someone is reading a guide, opening an app, or looking for a quick answer, Gide aims to provide help that feels clear, calm, and useful.

Looking Ahead

Gide is still growing.

New apps, books, tools, and resources may be added over time as the Gide family expands.

The long-term goal is to build a trusted collection of simple digital tools that help people live wiser, work smarter, stay safer, and move forward with confidence.
