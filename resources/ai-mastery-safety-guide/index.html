AI Mastery & Safety

Get better results. Protect what matters.

Use this checklist whenever you work with an AI tool.

Prompt clearly

State your goal, audience, constraints, and desired format. Give relevant context without sharing private information.

Verify carefully

Review facts, calculations, quotations, and links before relying on or publishing the result.

Protect your data

Never enter passwords, account numbers, private records, or confidential work unless the system is approved for it.

Watch for bias

Ask what perspectives may be missing and whether the answer treats people fairly.

Keep human control

Use AI to support decisions—not to replace judgment in health, legal, financial, or safety matters.

Pause when unsure

If an answer seems alarming, manipulative, or too good to be true, stop and consult a trustworthy source.
