1. Understand

Learn what AI can do, where it can fail, and why human judgment still matters.

2. Experiment

Start with a low-risk task such as brainstorming, outlining, or rewriting your own text.

3. Verify

Check important claims against reliable sources. AI output can sound certain while being wrong.

4. Protect

Keep passwords, financial information, health records, and other sensitive data out of prompts.

5. Apply

Build a small repeatable workflow that saves time without giving up oversight.
