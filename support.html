Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

What is Gide?
Gide is a growing family of apps, books, guides, and resources created by Trusted Stranger to provide practical guidance, support, protection, encouragement, and AI education for everyday life.
Which apps are part of Gide?
Gide currently includes apps focused on safety, calm, faith, and progress. These include Gide Guard, Gide Calm, Gide Faith, and Gide Stride. More apps may be added over time.
Do I need an account?
Most Gide apps are designed to be simple and accessible without requiring users to create an account.
Are Gide apps free?
Some Gide apps may be free, while others may include a one-time purchase, paid download, or optional premium features.
What are the AI books and guides?
Gide includes plain-English AI books and resources designed to help people use AI wisely, safely, and productively at work and in everyday life.
Where can I get help for a specific app or book?
You can contact support using the email below. Please include the app, book, or resource name, your device type if relevant, and a brief description of the issue.
Is my information shared?
Gide does not sell your personal information. Please review the Privacy Policy for more details about how information is handled.
